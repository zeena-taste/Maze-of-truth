Mask of Truth

Game Controls & Instructions

Action Keyboard On-Screen Description
Move Up ↑ or W ▲ Button Navigate upward in the maze
Move Down ↓ or S ▼ Button Navigate downward in the maze
Move Left ← or A ◀ Button Navigate leftward in the maze
Move Right → or D ▶ Button Navigate rightward in the maze
Zoom In + + Button Increase zoom level (up to 2x)
Zoom Out − − Button Decrease zoom level (down to 0.5x)
Reset Zoom 0 0 Button Return to normal zoom (1x)
Toggle Mask M Toggle Mask Button Turn the mask effect on/off (reveals treasures)

Objective: Navigate through the maze to find treasures when the mask is active. Use zoom to explore large mazes more easily.
